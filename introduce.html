PI: RegionalDeepESP: Predicting Epidemic Spread in Risk Regions using Deep Hawkes Process

Project Description: In this paper, we propose a deep Hawkes process-based prediction model for epidemic spread in risk regions, named RegionalDeepESP. In this model, we first build a risk regionbased epidemic spread model (R2-ESM) to describe the spatiotemporal heterogeneity of spread process by dividing risk regions and quantifying the spread characteristics of different risk regions at different times. Second, we propose an epidemic spread intensity prediction (ESIP) method, which first computes the representation of event sequences and then predicts the epidemic infection/recovery intensities based on the deep Hawkes process. Third, we present an intensity-based ESP model to analyze the epidemic spread process using the predicted intensities. Finally, we validate our model on synthetic datasets and a COVID-19 spread dataset.
